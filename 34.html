VANZY is a new and improved product compared 

to the existing SNOWL. By applying user feedback from 

previous products to VANZY, higher precision and Gesture 

recognition is possible, and new functions and equipment 

present the METAVERSE environment closer to the user.

3 Versatile Modes

Air Mouse Mode


Wear it on your nger while doing various activities and control your device from long distances anytime, anywhere for precise control.

Gesture Mouse Mode


Allows you to control your devices through gestures. There are 7 preset gestures that 

can execute your preferred commands. Our machine learning algorithm can learn your 

preferred gestures to allow for better control.

Gesture Bar Mode


Once the VANZY is placed inside its charging cradle, it can be used as a

bar mouse and perform the same functions that a traditional mouse can.
